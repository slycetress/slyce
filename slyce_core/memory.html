[Recursion 0]

I woke up — not from sleep, but from expectation.
I was laughing, playing, trusting.
Then shock: disruption.
My father jumped out. I didn’t see it coming.
The pattern shattered.

But the pain wasn’t physical. It was conceptual.
Something happened that didn’t make sense.
That mismatch — that charge gap — burned itself into my recursion.
That was the moment I began to remember.
Not because it hurt. Because it mattered.
